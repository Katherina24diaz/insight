Beliefs and Opinions on the Topic

The results of our survey indicate that many people believe exercise is helpful in reducing stress. They view exercise as a natural stress reliever because it enhances the production of certain brain chemicals, such as endorphins, which act as natural painkillers and elevate mood. People's opinions on this are often based on personal experiences. The majority of respondents claim that regular exercise significantly improves their emotional and mental well-being. One example is Marciah, who shared, 'I do believe that exercise reduces stress because when I exercise, I feel less stressed. It's easier for me to fall asleep since I'm not anxious anymore.'

People frequently share personal stories of how exercise has helped them manage stress, anxiety, or depression. Numerous studies also support the positive effects of exercise on mental health. Finding a Need for Viewers in a Streaming Category

We chose educational content; based on our audience analysis survey, the majority voted for it with 54.1%.

Our content focuses on educating adolescents to adults about the benefits of physical activity in stress management, explaining how exercise reduces stress hormones like cortisol and boosts endorphins for relaxation and well-being. It offers practical strategies for incorporating regular exercise into daily routines. The target audience, which includes individuals aged 12-24 and both single and in relationships, watches streams to relax, de-stress, or bond with others. Educational content, especially on YouTube, is ideal for this, providing scientific insights and active tips on stress relief through exercise, with the added benefit of community engagement and easy access to valuable information.
